Music as 7th Pyramid

This is my first alias and my main one right now. I focus on the faster side of dance music, especially on Drum & Bass with Jungle details, some Halftime, and a lot of atmospheric and melodic parts. Basically I don't usually go hardcore on my tracks, my background in pop/soul/funk music always gets me into the Liquid part of the genre.

Before focusing on those, I also made some House/Techno-ish stuff at 12X bpm, some dubstep beat stuff and other kinds of things, even Hip Hop.
